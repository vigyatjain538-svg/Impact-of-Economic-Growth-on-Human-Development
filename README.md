# 📊 GDP vs Human Development Analysis

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes the relationship between economic growth and human development in India using correlation analysis. It examines whether increases in GDP translate into improvements in quality of life, measured through the Human Development Index (HDI).

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Evaluate the relationship between GDP and HDI  
- Analyze whether economic growth leads to human development  
- Derive insights for policy and economic decision-making  
- Apply statistical techniques to real-world macroeconomic data  

---

## 📈 Key Findings
- Correlation coefficient (r) ≈ **0.198**  
- Indicates a **very weak positive relationship** between GDP and HDI  
- Economic growth does not strongly translate into human development

## 📊 Interpretation
- Growth alone is insufficient for improving living standards  
- Policy focus should include:  
- Education  
- Healthcare  
- Social welfare  
- Supports the **“beyond GDP”** approach in development economics :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
